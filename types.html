Types of Comedy

Although there are many more types of comedy, there are seven common versions of comedy.

slapstick

Slapstick is physical comedy and is one of the oldest forms of comedy. Slapstick became popular during the silent film era. One of the greatest silent film actors, Charlie Chaplin, was a slapstick comedian. One of the most popular of slapstick comedy is found in the movie "Home Alone"

Lampoon

Political comedy, making fun of figures of authority or the governments. "The Great Dictator" is an example of lampoon as it makes fun of Hitler.

romantic comedy

Romantic Comedy, or romcoms(but please don't call them that for the sake of your dignity) are annoyingly popular.
