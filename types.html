Types of Comedy

Although there are many more types of comedy, there are seven common versions of comedy.

Slapstick

Slapstick is physical comedy and is one of the oldest forms of comedy. Slapstick became popular during the silent film era. One of the greatest silent film actors, Charlie Chaplin, was a slapstick comedian. One of the most popular of slapstick comedy is found in the movie "Home Alone"

Lampoon

Political comedy, making fun of figures of authority or the governments. "The Great Dictator" is an example of lampoon as it makes fun of Hitler.

Romantic Comedy

Romantic Comedy, or romcoms(but please don't call them that for the sake of your dignity) are annoyingly popular. This form of comedy often revolves around the devolopment of two characters relationship. Parody

Mocking other genres or celebrity figures. It often uses sarcasm, stereotyping, and mocking of other scenes. "Blazing Saddles" is an old example of a parody.

Sophmoric

Sophmoric comedy is that would normally be expected for an adolecent. It's also known as juvenile humor. Sophmoric humor relies on toilet humor, intimate relationships humor, and gags that are made to appeal to a silly sense of immaturity

Gross Out

Humor that relies heavily on "toilet humor". "Dumb and Dumber is an example of a gross out film

Fish out of Water

This type of comedy derives humor from putting the main character in unexpected situation
